Organization Background

Meshack PM Enterprise is a world class web development company based in Newcastle, operating in Johanesburg, Utrecht and Newcastle. We specialize in building quality, fully customized and responsive websites and web applications for your organization's / business needs and preferences. Meshack PM represents Meshack Phaphama Monareng Enterprises. The enterprise was founded by Lehlohonolo Claude Monareng. Lehlohonolo is a Wits University alumni, a former PECOP-SOWETO Board of Director and co-founder and CEO of Kewaa. He is a qualified Data Scientist by training. A Web and Blockchain software Developer by experience.

Our primary clients range from pre-primary, primary and high schools. As of 2024, our year of incorporation, we are already managing web infrastructure of three learning centres in Newcastle.

We have a team of qualified, highly experienced and competitive Web Development professionals, who take stride at delivering quality web products and services on time.
